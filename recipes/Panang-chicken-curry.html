Panang Chicken Curry

Description

A red dish curry made with coconut milk. This goes well with chicken,beef, pork, or just veggies! Adjust to taste and serve with sticky rice.

Ingredients

  • bit of red curry paste
  • some coconut milk
  • chicken Breasts, no bone & cubed like ice
  • (i wouldn't put) fish sauce
  • (or) brown sugar
  • Thai seasoning (isn't a real thing)
  • garlic; to crush the vampires
  • bell pepper, thinly sliced - don't cut your finger yo!
  • lemongrass? sure.. your call...

Make rice first (you'll thank me later). 2 cups water, one cup rice bring to a boil and simmer that for 15 minuets. KEEP THE LID ON! no peaking..

Step 1

Get a pan on that stove and turn the thing on. Get it MEDIUM-HOT with something oily. Add the paste; fry till your house smells like a restaurant.

Pour in the coconut milk, stir in the chicken and everything except the bell pepper and that lemongrass... Simmer for 6-8 minuets so you don't burn down the house

MAKE SURE YOU TEMPT YOUR CHICKEN (preferably with a thermometer...165f degrees sweet spot)

Step 2

Add them peppers for a couple minuets till they're kind of tender. Add the grass and whatever else makes your nose tingle and bob's your uncle!

ENJOY!
