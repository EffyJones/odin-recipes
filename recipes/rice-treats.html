Rice Treat

Ingredients

  • much butter
  • mini/big (who cares) Marshmallows
  • much rice cereal fo real

Step 1

Melt that butter in a sauce pan over a low heat add some of those marshmallows and stir until its melted and well together.

Cook it a little longer for extra-ness. Remove from the heat.

Step 2

Stir in the cereal! You got this!

Step 3

Take a tiny bit of butter and rub it on a square looking pan. add the mix into that pan and press it down firmly.

Wait till it cools down or pop it in the freezer for a second.

Cut it however big ya want it.

Congratulations
